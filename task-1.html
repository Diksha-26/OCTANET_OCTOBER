VARIETIES

Rose

A classic choice symbolizing love and beauty, roses come in various colors and add elegance to any bouquet.

Daisies

Daisies are cheerful and come in various colors, making them a great choice for a bright and happy bouquet.

Lilies

These large, fragrant flowers are available in different types, including Asiatic, Oriental, and Calla lilies. They add a touch of sophistication.

Lotus

The lotus is a sacred flower in India, symbolizing purity and enlightenment. While it's not as common in everyday bouquets, it's sometimes used in special arrangements.

Tuberose

Tuberose is prized for its intense, sweet fragrance. It's often used in floral garlands and arrangements for special occasions.

Sunflower

These bold, sunny flowers are perfect for adding a pop of color and radiance to a bouquet.

Jasmine

Jasmine flowers are highly fragrant and symbolize purity and simplicity. They are often used in wedding bouquets.

Carnations

Available in numerous colors, carnations are long-lasting and add a lovely texture to bouquets.

Marigold

Known for its bright orange and yellow petals, marigolds are commonly used in traditional Indian ceremonies and festivals.
